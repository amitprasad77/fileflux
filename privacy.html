Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

🔒 Key Point: FileFlux processes all files entirely in your browser. Your files are NEVER uploaded to our servers. We cannot see, store, or access your documents.

1. Information We Collect

FileFlux is designed with privacy as a core principle. We collect minimal information:

  • Files: We do NOT collect, store, or process your files. All file processing happens locally in your browser using JavaScript.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized analytics data (page views, tool usage counts) to improve our service. This data contains no personal information.
  • Cookies: We use cookies only for functionality (e.g., remembering your preferences) and for advertising (Google AdSense, if applicable).

2. How Your Files Are Processed

All file operations on FileFlux — including PDF merging, splitting, compressing, and image conversion — are performed entirely within your web browser using client-side JavaScript libraries (such as PDF-lib.js and the HTML5 Canvas API). Your files never leave your device and are never transmitted to our servers.

3. Advertising

FileFlux displays advertisements provided by Google AdSense and similar ad networks. These services may use cookies and similar technologies to show you relevant ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalized advertising at adssettings.google.com.

4. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics — for anonymized usage statistics
  • Google AdSense — for displaying advertisements
  • Cloudflare — for hosting and CDN services

5. Data Retention

Since we do not collect your files or personal data, there is nothing to retain or delete. Anonymous analytics data may be retained for up to 26 months as per Google Analytics default settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to opt out of analytics tracking by using a browser extension such as uBlock Origin or the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on. You may also disable cookies in your browser settings.

7. Children's Privacy

FileFlux does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Our service is intended for general audiences.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
