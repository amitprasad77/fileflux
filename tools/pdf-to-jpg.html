Frequently Asked Questions

How do I convert a PDF to JPG? +
Drop your PDF file into the tool above, choose your quality setting, and click Convert. Each page of your PDF will be rendered as a separate JPG image that you can download individually or all at once.
What resolution will the images be? +
The "High" setting renders pages at 2x scale (typically 1190×1684px for A4). "Max" goes to 3x for print-quality output. Higher scale means larger, sharper images but slower processing.
Can I convert PDF to PNG instead of JPG? +
Yes — use the Output Format dropdown to choose JPG, PNG, or WEBP before converting.
