Frequently Asked Questions

How do I merge PDF files for free? +
Simply select or drag your PDF files into the tool above, arrange them in the desired order, and click "Merge PDFs". Your merged PDF will be ready to download instantly — no account required.
Is there a limit on how many PDFs I can merge? +
On the free plan, you can merge up to 20 PDF files at once. For unlimited merging and larger file sizes, consider our premium plan.
Are my PDF files kept private? +
Yes — 100%. FileFlux uses PDF-lib, a JavaScript library that runs entirely in your browser. Your files never touch our servers. We have zero access to your documents.
Can I reorder pages before merging? +
Yes! After selecting your files, you can drag and drop them to reorder. The final merged PDF will follow the order shown on screen.
