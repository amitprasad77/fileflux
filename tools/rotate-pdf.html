Frequently Asked Questions

How do I fix upside-down PDF pages? +
Select your PDF, choose 180° rotation, apply to "All Pages", and click Rotate. This will flip all pages right-side up.
Can I rotate just some pages? +
Yes — you can apply rotation to all pages, only odd pages, or only even pages. For individual page rotation, use the page-specific controls on each page.
