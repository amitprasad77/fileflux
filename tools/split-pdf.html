Frequently Asked Questions

How do I split a PDF into separate pages? +
Upload your PDF, choose "Split All Pages", and click Split. Every page becomes its own PDF file that you can download individually.
Can I extract specific pages from a PDF? +
Yes! Use "By Page Range" to define ranges like 1-3, 5, 8-10 — each range becomes its own file. Or use "Pick Pages" to select individual pages.
Is my PDF file private? +
Completely. All processing happens in your browser using PDF-lib. Your file never leaves your device.
