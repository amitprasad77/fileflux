Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I compress an image? +
FileFlux can reduce image sizes by 30–90% depending on the original file and quality setting. WEBP format generally achieves the best compression. A 5MB JPG can often be reduced to under 500KB with no visible quality loss.
Is my image uploaded to a server? +
No. FileFlux processes everything directly in your browser using JavaScript. Your images never leave your device, ensuring complete privacy.
What image formats are supported? +
FileFlux supports JPG/JPEG, PNG, WEBP, and GIF formats. You can also convert between formats during compression.
Can I compress multiple images at once? +
Yes! You can select multiple images at once or drag-and-drop a batch. All images are compressed simultaneously and you can download them all with one click.
Why compress images? +
Smaller images load faster on websites, save storage space, and are easier to share via email or messaging apps. Web images compressed to WEBP can cut page load times significantly, improving SEO and user experience.
