FAQ

What Word formats are supported? +
FileFlux supports .docx format (Word 2007 and later). The converter preserves text content, headings (H1-H6), paragraphs, bold, italic, underline, and basic list formatting.
Will images in my Word document be included? +
Basic text and formatting is preserved. Embedded images and complex layouts may not render perfectly in the browser-based converter. For complex documents with images, consider using Microsoft Word's built-in "Save as PDF" feature.
