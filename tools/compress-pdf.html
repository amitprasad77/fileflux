Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I compress a PDF? +
Results vary based on the PDF content. PDFs with many images can often be reduced by 50–80%. Text-heavy PDFs may compress 10–30%. Our High setting removes metadata, flattens layers, and reduces resolution of embedded images.
Will compression reduce quality? +
Low and Medium settings preserve print quality. High compression is best for sharing online where maximum file size reduction is more important than pixel-perfect quality.
Is my PDF file uploaded anywhere? +
No. FileFlux runs entirely in your browser. Your PDF never leaves your device.
