Frequently Asked Questions

Is an electronic signature legally valid? +
Electronic signatures are legally recognized in most countries under laws like the ESIGN Act (USA), eIDAS (EU), and similar regulations worldwide. For most business and personal documents, an e-signature carries the same legal weight as a handwritten signature. For highly regulated documents (e.g., wills, real estate deeds), check local requirements.
Is my PDF sent to your servers? +
Absolutely not. FileFlux eSign runs entirely in your browser using PDF-lib.js. Your document never leaves your device. We have zero access to your files at any point.
Can I sign multiple pages? +
Yes! Use the page navigation arrows to move between pages and place your signature on each page you need to sign. All placements are saved and included in the final downloaded PDF.
What types of signatures can I create? +
You can draw a signature with your mouse or touchscreen, type your name in a stylish handwriting font, or upload an image of your existing handwritten signature.
Can I sign on mobile or tablet? +
Yes — the drawing pad supports touch input, so you can sign naturally with your finger on a smartphone or stylus on a tablet.
