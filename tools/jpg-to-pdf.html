Frequently Asked Questions

Can I combine multiple images into one PDF? +
Yes! Select multiple images and they'll each become a separate page in a single PDF file. You can reorder them by removing and re-adding in the desired sequence.
What image formats are supported? +
JPG, JPEG, PNG, WEBP, GIF, and BMP are all supported. The images are embedded directly into the PDF.
Will image quality be reduced in the PDF? +
No — your images are embedded at full resolution. The PDF will look identical to your original images.
